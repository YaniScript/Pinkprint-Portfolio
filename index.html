YANIscript is...

YANIscript is my alter-ego in this web development realm. I am YANIscript and she is me. The name is a play on the scripting language Javascript and I made my GitHub repository when the pun came to me, so the rest is history. I wanted to change it to "YANAscript" so it sounds even more phonetically like Javascript but eff it, we ball! YANIscript is my exploration of who I am as a web developer. Right now, she is raw; she is unpolished. Politically incorrect, if you will. But she will be the best to ever do it! So YANIscript will be a commemoration of now and then (from a futuristic point of view.) I just want to document the journey so I can look back and say, "Yeah, Yani - you did that...and that, and that!"
